About

• From a young age, I developed a strong attraction to computers, beginning to browse the internet as soon as I learned to read and write. This early exposure sparked a deep interest in all things related to the digital world. Over time, this fascination evolved into a comprehensive understanding of the internet and its technologies.

• I began my journey into programming at 16, initially as a hobby to fill my free time. However, what started as a simple pastime quickly grew into a passion. I began by creating basic websites and gradually progressed to more complex projects, such as interactive stories using Twine and simple games developed in Scratch.

• At 20, I sought to deepen my programming skills and turned to Python, learning through YouTube tutorials and free online courses. My early projects, while not as polished as my current work, were crucial stepping stones in honing my abilities. In 2023, I enrolled in a 10-month online Python course, which I completed in 2024. This formal training helped me fill in knowledge gaps and further solidify my expertise.

• In addition to Python, I ventured into game development using Godot and GDScript in 2022, creating several games as part of my learning process. These experiences have broadened my skill set and enabled me to take on a wide range of projects.

• Looking ahead, I am excited to work on new ventures, including developing a website that leverages AI to assist users in decision-making and creating a mobile app designed to help people manage their time, workouts, and meetings more effectively.
